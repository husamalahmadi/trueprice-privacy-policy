Privacy Policy for TruePrice

Effective Date: May 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the TruePrice mobile app handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

TruePrice does not collect personal data. We may access publicly available stock information through external APIs and collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Information

The data accessed is used only for displaying live stock prices, calculating fair value, and showing financial ratios. We do not store or sell your data.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any personal information. The app may access public financial data from third-party APIs but no user-identifiable information is shared or processed.

4. Children's Privacy

TruePrice is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone.

5. Changes

We may update this policy. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
